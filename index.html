Our Vision

Our vision is to redefine the spirit of celebration. We dream of a world where every festival spreads happiness without harming the environment, where tradition meets sustainability and joy becomes an act of care for the planet.

We want to inspire people to celebrate festivals, birthdays and weddings in eco-friendly ways with less waste and more love. Through small creative actions, we hope to build a community that celebrates responsibly and sustainably.

Our Mission

At Team EarthUtsav, we empower youth and communities to ensure that tradition and sustainability walk hand in hand. From makeshift coconut candles to wedding veils for underprivileged brides, we spread awareness about environmental impacts and promote sustainable alternatives at every step of the way.

Through education, collaboration and shared responsibility, we strive to achieve our vision of sustainability and tradition existing in the same bubble of love, where joy becomes an act of care for our planet.

Our Theme: Eco-Friendly Celebrations

Eco-friendly celebrations are all about enjoying festivals, birthdays, weddings, and other special moments in ways that don't harm the planet. Instead of using plastic decorations, chemical-filled colors, or firecrackers, we believe in celebrating using biodegradable materials, reducing waste, and making sustainable choices.

Our message is simple: festive cheer today shouldn't cost the Earth tomorrow.

Team EarthUtsav chose this theme because while celebrations bring happiness, they often leave behind a trail of waste and pollution. After festivals like Diwali or Holi, air quality drops drastically, and lots of non-biodegradable waste piles up in landfills. Through our efforts, we hope to inspire everyone to celebrate consciously, by reusing decorations, saying no to single-use plastics, and switching to greener alternatives.

Our Activities

At Team EarthUtsav, our activities bring this vision to life. Each project encourages people to celebrate sustainably, think creatively, and care for the planet while spreading happiness.

01

Dupatta of Blessings

Repurposing temple dupattas into wedding veils for underprivileged brides.

Purpose: Reduces textile waste while spreading love and dignity through sustainable giving.

Impact: Encourages responsible reuse and inspires compassionate, waste-free celebrations.

02

Flower Dhoops

Turning discarded temple flowers into fragrant incense sticks.

Purpose: Prevents flower waste from polluting rivers and encourages mindful reuse of natural materials.

Impact: Reduces water pollution and supports sustainable livelihoods.

03

DIY Candles

Creating candles using leftover wax, coconut husks, and nut shells.

Purpose: Promotes creativity and teaches how to recycle waste into something beautiful and useful.

Impact: Cuts down landfill waste and inspires everyday sustainable habits.
